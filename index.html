Villa Sirena

Villa Sirena is a Mexican style villa located in El Pueblo, a small gated community with approximately 20 homes. Located about 10 minutes from Kralendijk on the North end of the island. All the homes have a stunning view of the Ocean with western exposure revealing beautiful sunsets. Within walking distance of dive and snorkel sites; the location is truly magnificent. Villa Sirena has 3 bedrooms each with its own ensuite bathroom. 2 bedrooms have kingsize beds ( beds can be split into 2 singles ) and the 3rd bedroom has a queen size bed. Each bedroom has its own air-conditioner. Open concept living room and kitchen with a large wooden dinning table out on the porch. Open the sliding doors, outside and inside will be one large area catching the tropical breezes. Comfortable spacious covered porch and a private swimming pool are surrounded with tropical gardens. Stairs lead to a magnificent roof terrace, a perfect place to watch the sunset and stars at night. For scuba divers we also have an outside shower, a rinse tank and storage area for scuba gear. Villa Sirena will be ready for rent in January 2019. Please check back for updates and pictures.
